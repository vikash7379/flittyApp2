These places in Delhi SCREAMS "luxury".

Ah, Delhi – the land of luxury! Delhi is a city of glamour, luxury and extravagance! If you're looking for an experience that screams elegance and opulence. What better way to indulge in the finer things than to explore some of Delhi's most extravagant places? From expensive spas and hotels to the finest clubs and restaurants, Delhi is sure to have something that will make you feel like royalty. If you're looking for a luxurious time, Delhi is the place to be! So, ready to join the rich and posh crowd? Let's take a look at some of the most exclusive spots in Delhi!

Bombay Club

Have you ever been to a place where you can sit back, take a deep breath and actually relax? The vibe of this restaurant is straight out of a dream, with its white interiors, minimalist setup, and plenty of daylight– it is the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of the city.

For the ardent foodie, Bombay Club Delhi offers an inviting menu of mouth-watering delicacies from Mumbai, that screams of authenticity and adds to the complete experience of dining. From the Vada Pav, Tawa Roti and Pani Puri from street food heaven to the delectable Seafood Thali that is sure to tantalise your taste buds, the menu covers all the popular dishes that one would expect from a great Mumbai eatery – and more!

So what are you waiting for? If you’re looking to add a li’l bit of Bombay to your life, rush to the Bombay Club. The place is perfect for Delhi’s rich and sophisticated crowd to take their family and friends along and enjoy a memorable treat. Get ready to be wow-ed and spread the word, because this new entrant in Dhan Mill Compound deserves a visit!

The Lodhi Aman Resorts

When you think of a staycation, The Lodhi stands as the perfect choice. Set in the midst of lush Lutyens Delhi and surrounded by the picturesque Lodi Gardens, The Lodhi allows you to experience the vibrant culture and exhilarating experiences that this dynamic city has to offer.

But if you’re really looking to bump up your staycation game, you can’t beat The Lodhi and its Les Clefs d’Or Concierges. Ready and waiting to show you the delights of Delhi, from tours of its iconic monuments, to in depth explorations of the culinary delights of the city, even down to chartering a private jet for some last minute shopping and theatre trips, these globetrotting concierges make the true luxury of The Lodhi clear.

And what better way to round off the perfect staycation than by dropping in for lunch or dinner at The Bombay Club Delhi, down the road from The Lodhi. Glamorous, decadent and ever-so-slightly witty, their elegant British Raj inspired dining room with its food, fizz and cocktails is proof that even Delhi’s rich and famous know how to enjoy life.

So come on, why not make The Lodhi your next staycation destination and let us show you why this spectacular city deserves its reputation as a modern-day renaissance.

Aura Heritage Spa

Aura Heritage Spa, a tranquil oasis in the heart of the city. Step into a space where tranquillity encompasses you and reality and fantasy blend in harmony. Here, wellness is all around, time stands still, and a deep sense of relaxation washes over.

Aura Heritage Spa specialises in providing personalised journeys towards relaxation. It believes that their guests deserve to be enveloped in a haven of serenity, and that’s exactly what they offer.

Their experienced team of professionals is dedicated to providing the ultimate spa experience. Our treatments are designed to help you relax, rejuvenate, and restore balance to your body and mind. Whether you’re looking for a massage, facial, body scrub, or something else, we’ve got you covered.

For those seeking an extra dose of relaxation, they offer a range of spa packages. From romantic couples’ packages to special spa days for groups, they’ve got something for everyone. So if you’re looking for a little R&R, Aura Heritage Spa is the perfect place for you.

Le Cirque

Le Cirque, Delhi's premier destination for French-Italian fine dining. They offer a truly unique experience, combining the best of both worlds. Their chef's tasting menu is designed to take you on a journey of exquisite flavours and textures, from the classics of French cuisine to the delicate nuances of Italian cooking.

At Le Cirque, dining is an experience. Their avant-garde bar, exclusive walk-in wine cellar, interactive chef's table and private dining rooms provide the perfect backdrop for a luxurious evening.

Their chef's menu is a must-try. Start your culinary adventure with Le Cirque Garden Vegetables with Truffle Dressing, a dish that will tantalise your taste buds. Move onto Ravioli stuffed with Barolo Braised Duck Leg with Black Truffle, a classic Italian dish with a unique twist. And finish with Paupiette of Chilean Sea Bass, a dish that is sure to leave you wanting more.

Noir Salon

Welcome to Noir Salon at The Chanakya – a luxury salon with a sophisticated and trendsetting edge. From the moment you walk through the doors, you’ll be enveloped in a world of polished interiors, comfy massage chairs, and the most amazing stylists.

Noir, pronounced as ‘Nua’, is a salon like no other. They specialise in bespoke services that are tailored to the individual – from hair spas, Kera therapy, instant conditioning, intensive nourishment, densifying and hair colouring, to name a few. Their range of services is designed so that you’ll always leave feeling pampered and refreshed

Whether you’re after a classic cut or something a little more daring, they’ll make sure you’re looking your best. They also offer a range of beauty treatments, from facials and massages to waxing and manicures.
