Your Luxe guide for dining, drinks and partying in Aerocity.

When it comes to luxury, there's no place quite like Aerocity in Delhi. This stylish neighborhood is home to some of the best restaurants, hotels, and nightlife in the city. If you're looking for a posh place to eat, drink, and be merry, here are some of the best spots in Aerocity to check out.

From Michelin-starred restaurants to chic bars and clubs, there's something for everyone in Aerocity. Aerocity is a great place to find food from all over the world. There are many restaurants that offer a variety of cuisines, so you're sure to find something to your taste. Here are five of our favorite places to eat in Aerocity:

Plum by Bent Chair:

This restaurant offers a unique dining experience with its quirky furniture and decor. The food is just as unique, with a mix of Indian and international dishes. Not only you can enjoy meals but also shop for some trendy and chic furniture. Talk about multi-tasking!

One8 Commune:

One8 Commune made it to the faves of Virat Kohli, need we say more? This is a great place to go if you want to try a little bit of everything. The menu offers a variety of cuisines, including Indian, Italian, Chinese, and more.

La Roca:

Perfect drinks, vintage decor, classy aesthetic, live music; what else can one ask for! This is a perfect place to get authentic Italian food. The pasta is made fresh and the pizzas are delicious.

Reve

Reve is a chic European themed restaurant with a very calm and cozy vibe. Reve serves a splendid variety of Italian and Continental cuisine along with a wide variety of drinks.

Grab a Negroni, a sbagliato, with prosecco in it
Looking for a place to get your drink on in Aerocity? Delhi's Aerocity is home to some of the best bars and restaurants in the city. If you're looking for a great place to grab a drink, here are some of our top picks:

Dragonfly:

  • This bar offers a unique selection of cocktails and beer, as well as a menu of small plates and shareable dishes.

  • Must try these: Hanky Panky, Negroni, Ultima, Green Beauty, Winter is Here, Moscow Mule

Liv Bar:

  • LIV Bar's idea is unique and the first of its type in Delhi NCR; it is a cozy martini BAR without a cocktail menu. Guests can visit the Bar and interact with our expert mixologists, selecting their chosen ingredients, fruits, tastes, and alcohol to create their own cocktails.


  • Must Try: Kamakaze shots, Say thank you Martini, Coffee Negroni, Delhi To Manhattan.

Mesa Wine Bistro:

  • This relaxed bistro is a great place to enjoy a glass of wine or beer. They offer a menu of small plates and shareable dishes, making it perfect for groups.

Nook:

  • Nook is a perfect place for wine lovers. They offer a wide variety of gourmet dishes which goes well with their extravagant wine collection.


  • Must try: Cloudy bay, Jacob’s creek, chardonnay and Pinot noir

The Beer Cafe:

  • The Beer Café has established itself as a pioneer in the alcoholic beverage industry. It is India's fastest growing alcoholic beverage chain, with over 40+ stores across the country and a continuous growth rate. The Beer Café has developed as one of the most known and awarded brands in the F&B industry, with state-of-the-art decor, prime locations, a vast selection of brews, and wonderful food matching.


  • So whether you're looking for a place to grab a quick pint or you want to spend the night indulging in some delicious cocktails, Aerocity has you covered. Cheers!

The hong kong club

  • The Hong Kong Club in Aerocity Delhi has quickly become one of the most popular nightlife destinations in the city. Offering a unique blend of Chinese Cantonese cuisine, Zodiac cocktails, live music and DJ performances, and a stunning Sunday brunch, the Hong Kong Club is the perfect place to unwind.


  • The Hong Kong Club is open till 4am, making it the perfect place to enjoy a late night out. The menu features a wide variety of Chinese Cantonese dishes, as well as Zodiac cocktails which are said to be lucky for your Zodiac sign. The live music and DJ performances are top notch, and the Sunday brunch is not to be missed.

Keya

  • This popular nightclub has been a staple in the South Delhi market for years, and it's easy to see why. With its outdoor seating and classic indoor nightclub decor, Keya Club is the perfect place to spend a night out on the town. And their cocktails are simply unbeatable. Their signature items are particularly popular among locals and visitors alike.


  • Though Keya Club doesn't stay open until the wee hours of the morning, it still easily earns a spot on our list for its intense nightclub feel. If you're looking for a nightlife experience that you won't soon forget, Keya Club is the place to be.

Toy Room

  • Toy Room Club is one of the most exclusive places to hang out for arts, fashion, and celebrities in the heart of Delhi. Being part of the beating heart of Soho has great significance for it, and it is committed to being a positive space with a diverse offering, changing and adapting to suit the needs of the era.


  • The design of the club takes visual and stylistic cues from a mix of sources: iconic children’s toys, fictional characters, comic book illustrations, contemporary popular media, celebrity lifestyle, and the counterculture of graffiti. All of these elements come together to create a unique and dynamic atmosphere that is perfect for having a good time.
